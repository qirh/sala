My Queue

MyQueue was also a super fun Android project that I worked on in the beginning of 2017. Moreover, useres will also be able to add movies and TV shows to their queue, which will be synchornized in the clud using Google's firebase. Git repo here: https://github.com/qirh/MyQueue
